Gift Certificate Policy

Gift certificates are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase and are redeemable only for the service or dollar amount specified at the time of purchase. After 12 months, any remaining value converts to the original monetary amount paid and may be applied toward a service of equal value. Service-based gift certificates cannot be divided into multiple visits or split into shorter sessions.

Gift certificates cannot be redeemed for cash and are not valid with other promotions or discounts. If the total cost of services exceeds the certificate value, the recipient is responsible for paying the difference at the time of service. Gratuity is not included.
